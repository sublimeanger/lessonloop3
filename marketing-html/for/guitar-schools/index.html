    Frequently asked questions

    Does LessonLoop support electric, acoustic, and bass guitar?
    Yes. You can set up any instrument type and track students' progress on each individually.
    Can I manage group guitar workshops?
    Absolutely. Schedule group sessions, track individual attendance, and bill each student or family.
    Does it work for a peripatetic guitar teacher?
    Yes. Whether you teach at home, in schools, or across multiple venues, LessonLoop handles multi-location scheduling.
    Can students share tabs and resources?
    Teachers can upload tabs, sheet music, backing tracks, and videos to the resource library. Students access them through the portal.
    Is there a free plan for solo guitar teachers?
    Yes. LessonLoop offers a free tier and a 30-day trial of all premium features. Perfect for getting started.
