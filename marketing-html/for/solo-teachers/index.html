    Frequently asked questions

    Is LessonLoop free for solo teachers?
    LessonLoop offers a generous free tier and a 30-day trial of all premium features. Solo teachers can run their entire studio on the free plan.
    Do I need technical skills to set up LessonLoop?
    Not at all. Sign up, add your students, set your schedule, and you're ready. Onboarding takes under 10 minutes.
    Can parents book lessons directly?
    Yes. Share your public booking page and parents can request trial lessons or regular slots based on your availability.
    Does it handle invoicing and payments?
    Yes — generate professional invoices in GBP, track payments, send reminders, and accept online payments via Stripe.
    Can I switch from spreadsheets easily?
    Yes. Import students and guardians via CSV. Most teachers are fully set up within 15 minutes.
