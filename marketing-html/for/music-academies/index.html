    Hours lost on invoicing

    Manual spreadsheets for termly billing across 50+ families is unsustainable.

    Student notes in one app, finances in another, attendance on paper — nothing talks to anything.

    No role boundaries

    Frequently asked questions

    Can LessonLoop handle multiple locations?
    Yes. Add unlimited locations, each with their own rooms, closure dates, and teacher assignments. All managed from one dashboard.
    Does it support team billing and payroll?
    Absolutely. Run bulk billing across all students, track teacher hours, and generate payroll reports by location or term.
    What roles and permissions are available?
    LessonLoop supports Owner, Admin, Teacher, Finance, and Parent roles, each with tailored dashboards and data access.
    Is there a parent portal?
    Yes. Parents can view schedules, pay invoices, track practice, and message teachers through a dedicated family portal.
    Can I migrate from another platform?
    Yes. We offer guided migration support. Import students, guardians, and lesson data via CSV or with our team's help.
