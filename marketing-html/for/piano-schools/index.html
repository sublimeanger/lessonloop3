    Frequently asked questions

    Can I track ABRSM and Trinity grades?
    Yes. LessonLoop supports all UK exam boards including ABRSM, Trinity, LCM, and RSL. Track current grade, target grade, and exam dates per student.
    Does it handle group piano lessons?
    Absolutely. Schedule group lessons with multiple students, track individual attendance, and bill each family separately.
    Can parents see their child's practice?
    Yes. Teachers set assignments and students log practice through the parent portal. Parents see progress charts and notes.
    Is it suitable for a small piano school?
    Perfect for it. Whether you have 2 pianos or 20, LessonLoop scales with your studio.
    Can I set different rates for different lesson lengths?
    Yes. Rate cards support per-duration pricing (30, 45, 60 min), sibling discounts, and scholarship rates. Billing runs apply the correct rate automatically.
