    Frequently asked questions

    What file types can I upload?
    PDFs, images, audio files, and documents. Upload sheet music, practice recordings, worksheets, or any teaching material.
    Can students access resources from the portal?
    Yes. Resources shared with a student appear in their parent portal. Parents and students can download them anytime.
    Can I organise resources by instrument or level?
    Yes. Tag resources by instrument, grade level, or custom categories. Filter and search to find what you need.
    Is there a storage limit?
    Storage limits depend on your plan. Solo includes basic storage, Pro and Academy include expanded limits.
    Can multiple teachers share resources?
    Yes. Resources can be shared at the organisation level. Teachers can access and assign shared materials to their students.
