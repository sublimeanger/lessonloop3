    The Problem

    Student info scattered everywhere

    Spreadsheets, notebooks, email threads — finding the right information about a student shouldn't require detective work.

    Can't find what you need

    Grade levels in one spreadsheet, contact details in another, lesson notes in your head. Nothing is connected.

    Guardian details out of date

    Phone numbers change, emails bounce, and you only find out when you need to reach someone urgently.

    No history at a glance

    How many lessons this term? What was covered last week? Impossible to answer quickly.

    The Solution

    A student profile designed for music educators

    Every feature built around how student records, instruments, and progress actually work in music education.

    Instruments & grades

    Track which instruments each student learns, their current grade, exam board, and full grade change history.

    Grade progression

    See a student's grade journey over time. Record exam results and track progress towards the next level.

    Guardian links

    Link one or more guardians per student. Store contact details and manage portal access from the profile.

    Lesson & attendance history

    Every lesson attended, missed, or cancelled — with dates, teachers, and notes.

    Custom notes & tags

    Lesson observations, medical notes, custom tags. Filter and search students by any field.

    GDPR-compliant records

    Data isolated per organisation with row-level security. Export or delete anytime for subject access requests.

    CSV bulk import

    Import students from CSV. Map columns, detect duplicates by email, preview before importing.

    Guardian portal badges

    See which guardians have activated their portal login and which are still pending.

    Make-up credits

    Credits tab showing balance, history, and expiry. Auto-issued on eligible absences.

    Frequently asked questions

    Can I store guardian/parent contact details?
    Yes. Each student can have one or more guardians linked to their profile, each with their own email, phone, and portal access.
    Can I track which instruments a student learns?
    Absolutely. Assign one or more instruments per student, each with their own grade level and exam board.
    Can I import students from a spreadsheet?
    Yes. Upload a CSV file and LessonLoop maps columns, detects duplicates by email, and lets you preview before importing.
    Is student data GDPR compliant?
    Yes. All student data is isolated per organisation with row-level security. Export or delete anytime.
    Can I add custom notes to a student profile?
    Yes. Each student profile has a notes section for lesson observations, medical notes, or anything else.
    How do parents access the portal?
    Add a guardian to a student profile and send a branded invitation email. Parents click through to create their account.
