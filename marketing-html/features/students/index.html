    Frequently asked questions

    Can I store guardian/parent contact details?
    Yes. Each student can have one or more guardians linked to their profile, each with their own email, phone, and portal access.
    Can I track which instruments a student learns?
    Absolutely. Assign one or more instruments per student, each with their own grade level and exam board.
    Can I import students from a spreadsheet?
    Yes. LessonLoop has a CSV import tool that maps your columns to student fields. Import hundreds of students in minutes.
    Is student data GDPR compliant?
    Yes. All student data is isolated per organisation with row-level security. You can export or delete student data at any time to comply with data requests.
    Can I add custom notes to a student profile?
    Yes. Each student profile has a notes section for lesson observations, medical notes, or anything else you need to remember.
