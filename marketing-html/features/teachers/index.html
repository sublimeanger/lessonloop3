    The Problem

    Managing teachers shouldn't mean more admin

    Coordinating availability, calculating pay, and controlling access — it's a full-time job on its own.

    Availability chaos

    Tracking who teaches when, where, and for how long — across multiple locations — using spreadsheets or WhatsApp.

    Payroll is manual

    Counting lessons, calculating hours, applying different rates — all done by hand every month. Errors are inevitable.

    No role separation

    Teachers can see other teachers' schedules, student details, or billing info they shouldn't have access to.

    The Solution

    Teacher tools built for music schools

    Every teacher gets their own profile, schedule, and role-based view — while admins get full visibility across the team.

    Availability management

    Teachers set weekly availability by day, time, and location. The scheduler respects these automatically.

    Pay rates & payroll

    Set hourly or per-lesson pay rates. Payroll reports calculate earnings from delivered lessons automatically.

    Multi-location assignment

    Assign teachers to one or more locations. Each location has its own rooms and availability windows.

    Role-based access

    Teachers see only their own students and schedule. Admins and owners see the full picture.

    Instrument specialisations

    Record which instruments each teacher covers. Match teachers to students by instrument and availability.

    Performance insights

    Track lessons delivered, cancellation rates, and utilisation per teacher. Spot trends and support your team.

    Role-Based Access

    Teachers see theirs. Admins see everything.

    Role-based access means teachers only see their own students and schedule. No accidental data exposure. No awkward billing visibility.

    Frequently asked questions

    Can teachers set their own availability?
    Yes. Each teacher defines their weekly availability blocks by day, time, and location. Admins can view and adjust availability across the team.
    Can I assign teachers to specific locations?
    Absolutely. Teachers can be assigned to one or more locations, each with their own availability and room access.
    Does LessonLoop track teacher pay?
    Yes. Set hourly or per-lesson pay rates for each teacher. Payroll reports calculate earnings automatically from delivered lessons.
    Can teachers see only their own schedule?
    Yes. Role-based access ensures teachers see only their own students, lessons, and schedules. Admins see everything.
    Can I invite teachers to join LessonLoop?
    Yes. Send an email invite with the teacher role. They sign up, set their availability, and start seeing their schedule immediately.
