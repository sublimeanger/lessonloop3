    The Problem

    Running blind costs you money

    Without clear reports, you can't see what's working, what's overdue, or where you're losing income.

    No revenue visibility

    How much did you earn this term? How much is outstanding? Without reports, you're guessing.

    Manual calculations

    Adding up lessons, calculating teacher pay, and tallying invoices in spreadsheets — every single month.

    Money slipping through

    Overdue invoices go unnoticed. Cancellation patterns aren't tracked. Revenue leaks you can't see.

    The Solution

    Reports that answer the questions that matter

    Every report is built from your real data — schedule, attendance, invoices, and payments. Always up to date, always accurate.

    Revenue reports

    Total revenue by term, month, teacher, or instrument. Compare periods and spot trends.

    Outstanding ageing

    Invoices overdue by 7, 30, 60, or 90+ days. Know exactly who owes what and for how long.

    Payroll reports

    Teacher earnings calculated from delivered lessons and pay rates. Export for your payroll provider.

    Lessons delivered

    Count lessons by teacher, student, instrument, or location. Track delivery against expectations.

    Utilisation & cancellations

    Room utilisation, teacher capacity, and cancellation rates. Optimise your schedule.

    Flexible filters

    Filter any report by date range, term, teacher, location, or instrument. Drill down to the detail.

    Frequently asked questions

    What reports are available?
    Revenue, outstanding invoices, payroll, lessons delivered, cancellation rates, teacher utilisation, and attendance trends.
    Can I export reports?
    Yes. Export any report as PDF or CSV for your records, accountant, or board meetings.
    Can I filter reports by date range?
    Absolutely. Filter by term, month, custom date range, teacher, location, or instrument.
    Are reports real-time?
    Yes. Reports pull from your live data. Revenue, attendance, and lesson counts are always up to date.
    Can teachers see reports?
    Teachers see their own payroll and lesson delivery reports. Revenue and financial reports are restricted to owners and admins.
