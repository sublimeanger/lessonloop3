    Frequently asked questions

    Everything you need to know about the parent portal and family access.

    What can parents see in the portal?

    Parents can view upcoming lessons, attendance history, invoices, payment status, practice assignments, and messages from teachers.

    Can parents pay invoices through the portal?

    Yes. Parents can view invoice details and make payments directly through the portal if Stripe is connected.

    Is the parent portal secure?

    Absolutely. Each parent has their own login. Row-level security ensures they only see data for their own children.

    Can parents message teachers?

    Yes. Built-in messaging keeps communication professional and in one place — no more scattered WhatsApp threads.

    How do parents get access?

    You invite parents by email. They receive a secure link, set a password, and are connected to their child's profile instantly.
