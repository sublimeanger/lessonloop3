    The problem

    Scheduling shouldn't be your second job

    Most calendar tools weren't designed for music teaching. You end up working around them instead of with them.

    Double bookings & conflicts

    Generic calendars don't understand teacher availability, room capacity, or student overlap — so clashes happen constantly.

    Hours lost to admin

    Setting up recurring lessons, handling reschedules, chasing confirmations — it all adds up to hours you should be teaching.

    Term dates are manual

    UK term dates, half-terms, bank holidays — most tools ignore them entirely, leaving you to manually skip or cancel.

    The solution

    A calendar designed for music teachers

    Every feature was built from real teaching patterns — not generic project management.

    Drag-and-drop rescheduling

    Move lessons between slots, days, and rooms with a single drag. Changes notify parents and sync instantly.

    Smart recurring lessons

    Weekly, fortnightly, or custom patterns. Auto-generates the whole term and skips your closure dates.

    Real-time conflict detection

    Clashes for teachers, rooms, and students are caught before you save. Never double-book again.

    UK term date engine

    Define your terms, half-terms, and bank holidays. Recurring lessons automatically skip blocked dates.

    Multi-location & rooms

    Manage multiple venues with individual rooms, availability windows, and closure dates from one view.

    Google Calendar sync

    Two-way sync keeps personal and teaching calendars aligned. Changes appear in Google instantly.

    Frequently asked questions

    Everything you need to know about LessonLoop's music lesson scheduling features.

    Can I set up recurring weekly lessons?

    Yes. Create a lesson once and LessonLoop generates every occurrence across the term automatically, respecting closure dates and holidays.

    Does LessonLoop detect scheduling conflicts?

    Absolutely. Real-time conflict detection prevents double-booking teachers, rooms, or students before you save.

    Can I manage multiple locations?

    Yes. Each location has its own rooms, availability, and closure dates. Switch between them from one calendar view.

    What happens when I reschedule a lesson?

    Drag-and-drop or edit the lesson. LessonLoop updates the calendar, notifies parents, and syncs with Google Calendar if connected.

    Does it work with UK term dates?

    Yes. Define your own terms, half-terms, and bank holidays. Recurring lessons automatically skip closure dates.
