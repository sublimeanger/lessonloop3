    The problem

    Chasing payments shouldn't be your second job

    Spreadsheets, manual calculations, and awkward payment conversations — billing is the part of teaching nobody signed up for.

    Hours wasted on manual invoicing

    Counting lessons, calculating totals, formatting invoices — it eats into evenings and weekends that should be yours.

    Overdue payments pile up silently

    Without automated reminders, unpaid invoices quietly stack up. Chasing payments feels awkward and unprofessional.

    GBP & VAT are afterthoughts

    Most tools default to USD with no VAT support. UK teachers need proper sterling invoicing with optional VAT — not hacks.

    Frequently asked questions

    Everything you need to know about LessonLoop's invoicing and billing features.

    Can I bill by term or by month?

    Yes. LessonLoop supports termly billing runs, monthly invoicing, and per-lesson billing. Mix and match across different students.

    Does LessonLoop handle VAT?

    Yes. Set your VAT rate (or zero-rate for exempt teachers). VAT is calculated and shown on every invoice automatically.

    Can I send payment reminders?

    Absolutely. Automated reminders go out before and after due dates. You can also send manual nudges with one click.

    What payment methods are supported?

    LessonLoop generates invoices in GBP. Parents can pay via bank transfer, or you can connect Stripe for card payments.

    Can I run bulk billing for the whole term?

    Yes. Select a term, preview every invoice, and approve the batch. Hundreds of invoices generated in seconds.
