    The Problem

    What happens between lessons matters

    You set homework, but have no idea if it was done. Students forget, parents don't know what to encourage, and progress stalls.

    No accountability

    Students forget what to practise. No record of what was assigned or completed.

    No visibility for parents

    Parents want to help but don't know what was assigned or how much is expected.

    Progress is invisible

    Without tracking, conversations about progress are based on gut feeling, not data.

    The Solution

    Practice tracking built for music education

    Assign practice, track time, and see progress — all connected to the student profile and parent portal.

    Practice assignments

    Create assignments with descriptions, targets, and due dates. Students and parents see them instantly in the portal.

    Practice logging

    Students or parents log sessions with duration, focus area, and notes. Built right into the portal.

    Streaks & progress

    Practice streaks and weekly totals keep students motivated. See who's putting in the work.

    Practice reminders

    Automated reminders nudge students and parents about upcoming goals and incomplete assignments.

    Teacher insights

    Review practice logs before each lesson. Know what a student worked on and how much time they spent.

    Linked to lessons

    Assignments connect to lesson dates. See what was assigned after each lesson and track completion.

    Frequently asked questions

    Can students log their own practice?
    Yes. Students (or parents on their behalf) can log practice sessions from the parent portal, including duration, what they practised, and notes.
    Can teachers set practice assignments?
    Absolutely. Teachers create practice assignments with descriptions, targets, and due dates. Students see them in the portal.
    Can I see how much a student has practised?
    Yes. Practice logs are visible on the student profile and in the parent portal. Teachers can review before each lesson.
    Does practice tracking work on mobile?
    Yes. The parent portal is fully responsive. Students and parents can log practice from any device.
    Is practice tracking included in all plans?
    Practice tracking is available on Pro and Academy plans.
